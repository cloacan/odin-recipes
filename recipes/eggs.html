The Best Eggs Your Momma Never Made

Description

Creamy, delicious eggs that melt in your mouth. The perfect pairing to any breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 6 Eggs
  • Butter
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Cream Cheese

Steps

  1. In an appropriately sized non-stick skillet, melt butter until liquid
  2. Crack all eggs directly into pan. Take a scoop of cream cheese and break it up into the pan, breaking the yolks of the eggs as you go.
  3. Using a wooded spoon, scramble the eggs on medium-low heat until almost cooked. Add shredded cheese to the and remove from heat. Allow cheese to melt completely on top of the eggs, they will finish cooking during this process.
  4. Serve alone or with our delicious Steak recipe.
