Cripy Oven Baked Hot Bacon

Description

This oven baked bacon provides an evenly cooked, crispy bacon that takes little work and produces consistent results with a hint of heat.

Ingredients

  • One package of bacon
  • Ghost Pepper Flakes

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Place bacon on a foil lined baking pan with a small space between each strip.
  3. Sprinkle desired amount of ghost pepper flakes over bacon.
  4. Cook for 10 to 15 minutes. Check every minute after the first 10 until desired doneness is achieved.
  5. Transfer from the pan to a paper towel lined plate to remove excess grease.
  6. Eat as a snack or enjoy with our Scrambled Eggs recipe!
