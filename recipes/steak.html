Steak

Description

This moist Ribeye steak will sure to be a family favorite. It's robust flavor will lead you to crave it multiple days a week.

Slowly cooking in the over and searing over high heat once the temperature has reached its apex, will ensure this steak can be enjoyed with an optimal eating experience

Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak
  • Butter
  • Steak Seasoning

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 250 degrees and place seasoned steak in a foil lined pan with a grate to heat evenly.
  2. Cook until internal temperature reaches 110 degrees
  3. Heat cast iron to medium high heat, melting butter as you heat it.
  4. Sear steak for one minute on each side, basting with butter from the pan during the second side.
  5. Let rest for 10 minutes on a cutting board and serve.
